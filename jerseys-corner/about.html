From the Family

A love letter
to the classics.

Jersey's Corner has been on the corner of Main Street since 1958. What started as a Sunday table — three generations passing platters, hot honey on everything, arguments over who makes the best mac — eventually became this: a building, a few booths, and a whole lot of regulars.

Our menu is eclectic on purpose. Burgers next to pastrami reubens next to gyros next to ribeye. We cook what we love, the way we love to eat it — made fresh, made right, made for you.

No heat lamps. No shortcuts. Just the recipes we grew up on, served the way they were always meant to be: hot, hearty, and a little bit personal.
